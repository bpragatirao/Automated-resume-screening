Using Transformer-based models (BERT / SBERT) significantly benefits from GPU acceleration.

Recommended GPU Options

GPU	VRAM	            Suitable Use
NVIDIA GTX 1660	6 GB	Fine-tuning small BERT models
NVIDIA RTX 2060	6 GB	Medium-scale NLP tasks
NVIDIA RTX 3060	12 GB	Ideal for this project
NVIDIA RTX 3080	10 GB	Large batch fine-tuning
NVIDIA Tesla T4	16 GB	Cloud / lab environments
Minimum VRAM: 6 GB
Recommended VRAM: 8–12 GB
